# Brand Guidelines

These guidelines apply across all products and experiences in Autumn Alexander's portfolio.

---

## Voice & tone

**Professional but human.** Not corporate-speak, not casual slang. The voice is:
- Confident — says what it means without hedging
- Clear — no jargon, no buzzwords
- Direct — gets to the point
- Warm — real estate is a people business; the tools should feel like a trusted advisor

### Do
- "Add a step" → "New step"
- "No items yet" → "Nothing here yet"
- "An error occurred" → "Something went wrong — try again"

### Don't
- "Leverage synergies" — jargon
- "Oops!" — too casual for a business tool
- "Please wait..." — passive; prefer showing progress

## Photography direction

*(For marketing, decks, social — not in-app)*

- Real spaces over staged photos
- Natural light, warm tones
- South Florida aesthetic: water, architecture, open sky
- People shown in authentic moments, never posed stock imagery
- Diverse representation across demographics

---

## Illustration style

Influenced by GitLab Pajama's illustration philosophy:
- Simple, geometric line work
- Single accent color per illustration (primary brand color)
- Used sparingly — empty states, onboarding, error pages
- Never decorative — every illustration communicates something
- Keep it at ~60px–120px in UI contexts; larger for marketing

## Color across products

Each product has a primary color but all share the same semantic token system:

| Product | Primary | Feel |
|---|---|---|
| OpsHQ | Indigo `#3E54D3` | Command, authority, clarity |
| CloserDojo | Amber `#D97706` | Energy, confidence, training |
| ListingHawk | Teal `#0D9488` | Sharp, precise, data-driven |
| Evergreen (brand) | Green `#16A34A` | Growth, nature, real estate |

---

## App icons & favicons

Each product icon uses the same geometric grid but with its own primary color and a unique symbol. All icons are built on a `1024×1024` canvas with a `120px` corner radius (following Apple/Google app icon specs).
